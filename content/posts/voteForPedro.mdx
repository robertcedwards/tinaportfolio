We built [Lynxify](https://lynxify.xyz "Lynxify") as part of Backdrop Build v4 and as part of a larger, long-term project.

Lynxify is the portal for your Hedera assets and the the rest of web3. Moving your tokens into Hedera has never been so easy. Enjoy the low fees and transaction speed of the Hedera network with your erc20 assets. In addition we will be adding Hedera and pool staking options bringing more of the features we are used in ERC for the Hedera blockchain.
